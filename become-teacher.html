Why Become a Teacher?

💪

Build Confidence

Teaching helps you become more confident and improve public speaking

🧠

Deepen Knowledge

The best way to learn something is to teach it!

⭐

Earn Recognition

Get badges, certificates, and special perks

🤝

Make Friends

Connect with other kids who share your interests

📋 Requirements

  • Age: Must be between 10-17 years old
  • Parent Permission: A parent or guardian must approve your application
  • Subject Knowledge: You should be really good at what you want to teach
  • Patience: Teaching requires being kind and patient with learners
  • Reliability: You must commit to your scheduled classes

Teacher Application
