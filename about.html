Our Story

Kids-Teach-Kids was born from a simple observation: kids learn best from other kids. When a child explains something to a peer, they use language, examples, and analogies that resonate on a whole different level.

Founded in 2026 by a group of educators and parents who saw the magic of peer-to-peer learning, our platform creates a safe, supervised space where young teachers can share their knowledge and young learners can discover the joy of learning from someone who "gets it."

Today, we're building a community of thousands of young minds across the country, all united by the belief that every child has something valuable to teach.
