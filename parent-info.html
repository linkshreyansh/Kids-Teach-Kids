Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure my child's safety? +
All sessions are monitored by adult supervisors. Classes are recorded and available for parents to review. All teachers have parent permission and are interviewed. We follow COPPA guidelines strictly.
What if my child wants to become a teacher? +
That's great! Kids can apply to become teachers with parent permission. We interview them, verify their knowledge, and provide training. Parents must approve all classes and schedules.
What ages do you serve? +
Learners can be ages 5-16. Teachers are ages 10-17. All classes are age-appropriate and grouped by similar age ranges.
How do payments work? +
We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Monthly subscriptions auto-renew but can be canceled anytime. Single classes are pay-per-class. Your first class is always free!
What if we need to cancel a class? +
You can cancel up to 24 hours before class for a full credit. Last-minute cancellations may not be refunded, but you'll get access to the recording.
Do you offer refunds? +
Yes! If you're not satisfied after your first paid class, we offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
