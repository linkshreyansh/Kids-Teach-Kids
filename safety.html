Safety Policies

📜 Teacher Code of Conduct

  • Be Respectful: Treat all learners with kindness and patience
  • Stay Professional: Keep conversations focused on learning
  • No Private Contact: All communication must happen on our platform
  • Follow Curriculum: Teach approved content only
  • Report Concerns: Immediately report any safety issues

📜 Learner Guidelines

  • Be Kind: Treat teachers and classmates with respect
  • Stay Engaged: Participate positively in class
  • Ask Questions: It's okay to ask for help
  • No Inappropriate Behavior: Follow the rules
  • Tell a Parent: Report any concerns immediately

📜 Parent Responsibilities

  • Supervise: Be aware of your child's online activities
  • Review: Check session recordings regularly
  • Communicate: Talk with your child about their classes
  • Report: Contact us immediately with any concerns
  • Stay Updated: Keep your contact information current

Frequently Asked Safety Questions

🔍 How do you vet teachers?

All teachers must have parent permission, complete an interview, and demonstrate their knowledge. We also monitor their first few classes closely.

👀 Are classes really monitored?

Yes! Every live session has an adult monitor present. All sessions are recorded and available for parents to review.

📱 What if I see something concerning?

Contact us immediately through our 24/7 emergency line. We have protocols in place to handle any situation.

🔒 How do you protect my child's privacy?

We are COPPA compliant. We never share personal information and all data is encrypted. Parents control their child's account.
